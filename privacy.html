Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 07, 2026

Introduction

Oneiros ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our service. We provide this policy to ensure transparency and compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and app store policies.

By using Oneiros, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

1. Personal Information

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you create an account via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In to authenticate you and manage your dream journal securely.

2. User-Generated Content

  • Dream Journal Entries: We store the text descriptions of dreams you submit for interpretation.

3. Usage Data

  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous data related to how the app is used (e.g., screens visited, session duration) to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To authenticate your account and store your dream journal across devices.
  • AI Interpretation: Your dream descriptions are processed by our AI service provider (Google Gemini) to generate interpretations. This data is sent securely and is not used to train public AI models.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage trends and fix bugs.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted third-party service providers required to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Uses simplified data for authentication, database storage, and analytics.
  • Google Gemini API: Processes dream text to generate interpretations.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data and dream journal entries only for as long as you maintain an account with us. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the app settings.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update your profile information within the app.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can delete your account and all data permanently via the "Delete Account" option in the Settings menu.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You may request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Oneiros is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
